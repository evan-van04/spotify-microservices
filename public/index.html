Spotify Microservices Dashboard

Explore five independent microservices built on top of the Spotify Web API: inspect a song’s stats, find similar tracks, analyze global trends, view user listening habits, and summarize playlist vibes.

Song Stats

Inspect tempo, energy, valence, danceability, and acousticness for a single track and get an overall mood label.

Song Similarity

Provide a track and get recommended songs with similar audio characteristics and vibe.

Trend Analytics

Retrieve currently trending tracks with high popularity scores and inspect why they’re popping.

Top Tracks

View a user’s top tracks based on their listening history and habits.

Playlist Analyzer

Paste a playlist and get an overall profile: average tempo, mood, energy and vibe labels.
